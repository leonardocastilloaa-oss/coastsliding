HOA and Commercial Sliding Door Repair in Miami-Dade, Broward & Palm Beach

Property managers need repairs that are fast, documented and repeatable. One stuck balcony door can become a resident complaint, a security issue or a water intrusion risk. Across South Florida, multi-unit buildings also face a predictable pattern: salt air wears rollers, tracks collect debris, locks drift out of alignment and aging impact glass begins to fail.

CoastSlide supports HOAs, condominium associations, apartment communities, hotels and commercial properties with sliding door, window, lock, track, roller and impact glass repair. We provide itemized per-unit notes, COI documentation, volume pricing and maintenance plans designed to reduce emergency calls.

Yes. Itemized per-unit invoicing is available for HOAs, condos and property managers.

Do you offer volume pricing?

Yes. Volume pricing is available for multi-unit work, typically starting around 10 units depending on scope.

Yes. We can perform scheduled inspections and identify patterns such as roller corrosion, track drainage problems or lock alignment issues.
