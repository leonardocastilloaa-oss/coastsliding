Why Doors and Windows Fail Differently in Palm Beach County

Palm Beach County repairs often involve heavier panels, cleaner finishes and higher expectations inside the home. A luxury sliding system needs careful handling: protect floors, tune the panel, match visible hardware and avoid rough adjustments that solve one symptom while creating another.

Estate homes, waterfront condos, golf community homes and premium impact windows all benefit from diagnosis before parts are replaced.

Repair Scenarios We See in Palm Beach County

The heavy balcony slider

The door still moves, but only with two hands. This is usually the moment to inspect rollers before the track is scored.

The lock that suddenly stopped working

Often the lock is not the first failure. Worn rollers lower the panel and the hook no longer meets the keeper cleanly.

The pre-storm glass concern

Cracked or fogged impact glass should be evaluated before hurricane season so owners have time to plan.
