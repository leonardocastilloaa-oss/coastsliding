About CoastSlide

Built on honest pricing, first-visit fixes, and marine-grade parts.

CoastSlide was founded by South Florida tradespeople who were tired of seeing homeowners overcharged, given vague estimates, and left waiting days for parts that "had to be ordered." We built CoastSlide on three commitments: show up on time, fix it on the first visit, and charge exactly what we quoted.

South Florida's coastal climate destroys standard door hardware faster than anywhere else in the U.S. We invested in marine-grade 316L stainless parts, HVHZ certifications, and bilingual technicians so every homeowner gets the same quality — regardless of what language they speak.

Today we serve from Homestead to Jupiter with the same commitment: fix it right, fix it once, and back it with a warranty.
